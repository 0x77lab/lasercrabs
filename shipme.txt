The Yearning
===========

Requirements
------------

- Windows 7+, OS X 10.11+, or some sort of linux...
- 2 GB RAM
- GPU supporting OpenGL 4.1
- Xbox 360 controller recommended

Settings
--------

Settings are stored in the following locations:

- Windows: C:\Users\<user>\AppData\Roaming\PoorMan\Yearning\config.txt
- Mac: /Users/<user>/Library/Application Support/PoorMan/Yearning/config.txt
- Linux: /home/<user>/.local/share/PoorMan/Yearning/config.txt
